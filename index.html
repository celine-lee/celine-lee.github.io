Celine Lee
PhD candidate @ Cornell University, advised by Sasha Rush. I work on language modeling, with a special focus on code reasoning and generation.
Currently: Student Researcher @ Google Deepmind
I have worked with fantastic teams at IBM TJ Watson (mentored by Ramón Fernandez Astudillo), Intel Labs (mentored by Justin Gottschlich), and the University of Pennsylvania (mentored by Dan Roth). I devote energy to teaching and education; in Spring 2025, I was an Adjunct Instructor at the Mohamed Bin Zayed University of AI, and in Summer 2023 taught the Cornell's Break Through Tech Program.
