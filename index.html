Celine Lee
PhD student @ Cornell University, advised by Sasha Rush. I work on language modeling, with a special focus on code reasoning and generation.
Currently (Spring 2025): Adjunct Instructor @ Mohamed Bin Zayed University of Artificial Intelligence in Abu Dhabi, UAE.
Previously: BSE & MSE @ University of Pennsylvania, 2020, where I worked with Dan Roth and Justin Gottschlich.
My CV and my Google Scholar.

I am co-organizing the VerifAI: AI Verification in the Wild workshop @ ICLR 2025. Submit, review, and attend!
